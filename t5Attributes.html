Technology science, also known as technological science or engineering science, is a field that applies scientific principles to design and
create practical solutions. It focuses on developing functional systems and structures to meet human needs.
Unlike natural sciences, which focus on understanding the natural world, technology science is concerned with human-made objects and their functions.
